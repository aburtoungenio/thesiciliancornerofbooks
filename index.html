My "always you" option


As you may imagine, This is the review of what a bilieve is the best book ever written and published.
Is it fair?... guilty as charged, but you would've done the same.

The Name of The Wind is easily the best book I have ever read. It has the trill of a fantastic hero whose pursuit of innerpeace lets him a the will of the wind and the cruelty of love. The story starts with the owner of Waystone Inn, his pupile and a storyteller. This third convinces the owner to tell him his story. Being told from 2 different perspecives, it is easy to fall for the teller's charm while enjoying the best narrative of a kid who becomes a magician, killer, lover, and a sex legend. I had the best time of my life reading this book over and over again, and while it's the first of a trilogy, it's by far the most beatiful book in history.
